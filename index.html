--- layout: sidebar title: About Soheil Eshghi ---


I am a postdoc at Yale Institute for Network Science (YINS), and I am honored to work with Leandros Tassiulas.
I use optimal control and game theory to study social, biological, and engineered networks.
In particular, I am interested in understanding dynamic influence in networks.

I obtained a PhD at Penn under the tutelage of Saswati Sarkar and Santosh S. Venkatesh. My thesis was on the optimal control of epidemics in the presence of heterogeneity, showing how a more granular view of epidemic spread can be used to design better policies.
